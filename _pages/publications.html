My research spans applied microeconomics, entrepreneurship, financial access, and non-bank lending. I also advance empirical methods in causal inference, including difference-in-differences, semi-parametric estimation and inference, and causal machine learning. More recently, I have begun exploring how semi-parametric econometric tools can improve the evaluation of large language models.
